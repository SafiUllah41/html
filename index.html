Pakistani chapli kabab:

Chapli kebab is a minced meat
patty widely popular in Pakistan. Mixed with
a blend of spices, ground beef is
flattened into patties and shallow-fried until
the exterior is charred and flavorful.
Serve in a bun with pickled red onions
or with a side of pita bread. My raita
(yogurt dip) recipe makes a great addition.

Prep time : 30 mins

cock time : 10 mins

total time : 40 mins

Ingredients:

  • cooking spay
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomato, sliced into rounds
